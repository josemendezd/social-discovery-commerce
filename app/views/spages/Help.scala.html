Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

This is a short list of our most frequently asked questions. For more information about Boozology, or if you need support, please contact us.

Boozology Related

What's Boozology

We're a community of people who love cool stuff. Here you can learn, discover, and talk about Boozy drinks and Booze related things.

How to sign up for Boozology

To sign up for Boozology, please go here . You can also go the homepage (www.boozology.com) and hit the "Sign Up" button on the top menu bar. You can register via Facebook, Google+, Twitter, or with an email address.

How to "love" or "like" an item.

To like an item, scroll over the item you love and click the "Like it" button that hovers over the image.

How to view items you have liked

To view items that you have liked, please select the "My Page" button located in the upper right hand corner of the page.

How to create and edit Collections

To add an item to a collection, scroll over the 'like it' button until it reads, "Collect". Select the collection you want to add the image to or create a new collection at the bottom. When you are finished, click the blue "Add to collection" button.

How to install the "Magic" button

To add things from other websites, drag the Magic button to your web browser.
If you're using Chrome, Safari or Firefox... -Turn on your Bookmarks toolbar from the View menu.
